About IIT Bombay Tech Fest

Tech Fest, IIT Bombay, is the annual science and technology festival of the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay. It is renowned as Asia's Largest Science and Technology Festival, attracting over 1.7 lakh visitors annually.

Our mission is to provide a platform for students to showcase their technical prowess, foster scientific temperament, and inspire the next generation of innovators.
